# 2023-02-15

## English
Smoke billowed from an Ohio train after a
derailment caused a fire that leaked five
carriages of the toxic chemical vinyl
chloride. Ohio residents found their
poultry and livestock suddenly dying.


## words
* billowed: 在波涛中翻滚
* derailment: 出轨
* toxic: 有毒的
* poultry: 家禽
* livestock: 家畜；牲畜
