# 2022-07-20


## English
Japanese figure skater Yuzuru Hanyu, 27,
has announced his retirement from
competitive competition. Two-time
Olympic champion Yuzuru Hanyu finished
fourth in the men's singles at the Beijing
Winter Olympics.
