# 2022-07-05


## English
Researchers at the University of California,
Irvine, have found that a signaling
molecule called SCUBE3 stimulates hair
growth and helps humans fight androgeninduced
hair loss.

## words
* signaling: 打信号，发信号
* molecule: 分子
* stimulate: 刺激
* androgen: 雄性激素（尤指睾酮）
* induce: 诱因
* androgen-induced: 雄性激素导致的
