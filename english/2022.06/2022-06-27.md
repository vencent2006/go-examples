# 2022-06-27


## English
On Friday, the Court overturned the 1973
ruling known as Roe v. Wade, sparking
protests that extend throughout the
weekend. Abortion-rights protesters 
continued to voice their fury and anguish
nationwide.

## words
* overturn: 推翻
* ruling: 判决
* spark: 点燃
* abortion: 堕胎
* fury: 愤怒
* anguish: 痛苦，令人辛酸
