# 2022-11-07

## English
A normal cup of milk tea contains about
3.5 cups of Americano. Caffeine has a 
refreshing effect, but in sensitive people,
caffeine consumption can cause insomnia, 
anxiety, irritability and other symptoms.

## words
* Americano: 美式咖啡
* caffeine: 咖啡因
* refreshing: 提神的
* insomnia: 失眠
* irritability: 烦躁
