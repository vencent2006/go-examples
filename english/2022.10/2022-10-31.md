# 2022-10-01

## English
While spending time with yourself, you
can do quite a few activities as well, such
as journaling, reflecting on your day,
simply sorting out your thoughts, and/or
expressing gratitude. Start practicing this
virtue today and one day you'll look back and
thank yourself for starting the practice.

## words
* journaling: 日记，日志
* reflect on: 反思
* sort out: 分类，整理
* gratitude: 感激，感谢
* virtue: 美德，德行，价值，长处
